#### Factors of Production
1. Land
	   all natural resources
	   receives **rent**
2. Labour
	   represents the work force
	   receives **wages**
3. Capital
	   produced means for further development
	   gets **interest**
4. Entrepreneurship 
	   refers to individuals who organize production and take risks
	   receives **profits**
